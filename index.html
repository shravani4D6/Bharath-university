Who We Are

Pioneering Academic Excellence For Over Four Decades

Bharath Institute of Higher Education and Research (BIHER) was established in 1984 to provide top-tier academic training to promising students. BIHER is committed to offering high-quality multi-disciplinary environments that promote research, innovation, and global placements.

We actively collaborate with international organizations and research centers, hosting over 10,000+ national and international students on our campus.
